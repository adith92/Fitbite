# Fitbite Roadmap

## Phase 0

Repo setup, Next app, docs, Vercel deploy.

## Phase 1

Customer core: body check, ingredient input, recipe UI, menu by program.

## Phase 2

Order core: cart, checkout, upload payment proof, admin order table, kitchen queue, delivery manual.

## Phase 3

Business report: sales, menu, program, member, kitchen, delivery, export CSV.

## Phase 4

AI recipe engine: recipe JSON, macro contribution, halal and local food preference.

## Phase 5

Automation: payment gateway, WhatsApp API, delivery integration, photo scan, voice input.
